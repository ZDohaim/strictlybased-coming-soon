About StrictlyBased

Welcome to StrictlyBased – the app that revolutionizes how you shop for groceries, personal care products, and more! In a world filled with overwhelming choices, StrictlyBased cuts through the noise to give you instant feedback on the products you use every day.

Our Mission

At StrictlyBased, we're dedicated to helping you make informed, conscious decisions about the items you bring into your life. Whether you're shopping for food, skincare, or household essentials, our mission is to empower you with clear, actionable insights based on ingredient transparency, health impact, and sustainability.

How It Works

StrictlyBased uses cutting-edge AI and image recognition technology to scan and analyze product ingredient lists. In just seconds, you'll know if an item is "based" (aligned with your values) or "unbased" (not quite right for you) – no guesswork required.

  • Scan It: Take a photo of the product's ingredient label.
  • Analyze It: Our AI reviews the data, cross-referencing it with our extensive database of research-backed criteria.
  • Get Results: See an easy-to-understand breakdown of whether the product passes the test.

We've designed StrictlyBased to be fast, reliable, and fun to use, so you can shop with confidence.
